African classic Cake Baking Guide

To bake a delicious african cake, please kindly follow these step by step

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups all purpose flour
  • 2.5 teaspoons baking powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups of refined brown suger
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract for the vanilla flavor
  • 1.5 cups of whole milk

Tools Needed

  • 2 x 9-inch round cake pans
  • Mixing bowls
  • Electric mixer
  • Whisk
  • Spatula
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Cooling rack
  • Parchment paper

Procedure to be followed

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line cake pans with parchment paper.
  2. Mix dry ingredients: In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cream butter and sugar: In a large bowl, beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy (3-4 minutes).
  4. Add eggs: Beat in eggs one at a time, then add vanilla extract.
  5. Alternate dry and wet ingredients: Add flour mixture in 3 parts, alternating with milk in 2 parts, beginning and ending with flour.
  6. Divide batter: Pour equal amounts into prepared pans and smooth tops.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  8. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.
  9. Frost with your favorite icing once completely cooled.
